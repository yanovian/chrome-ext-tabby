# Localization credits

Thank you to everyone who takes the time to check Tabby's localization and fix
wrong or awkward wording. This page credits who reviewed each language.

Want to help? If you're a native or fluent speaker of one of Tabby's languages,
open a pull request that reviews and corrects your language's strings. Extension
strings live in [`locales/`](../locales/), website strings live in
[`website/src/i18n/`](../website/src/i18n/). Once merged, your name goes here.
